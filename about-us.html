Our Environmental Mission

To offer a sustainable and eco-friendly way to sell your equipment.

By selling your equipment to CashForCameras, you will help us to:

  • Reduce the electronic waste reaching our landfills.
  • Reduce air pollution from new device manufacturing.
  • Conserve our planet’s valuable natural resources.

We responsibly re-use all shipping materials that are sent to us. When necessary, we purchase recycled and bio-degradable packaging materials. All usable device hardware is salvaged, inspected, tested and re-used. Unusable glass, metal and components are properly sorted and recycled.
