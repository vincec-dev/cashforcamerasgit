1. How does the process work?

Step 1 - Get A Cash Offer

Within 2 business days after completing our Cash Offer Form, you will receive an email from us that will include:

  • Our cash offer, valid for 30 days.
  • A FREE prepaid shipping label.
  • Shipping instructions.
GET A CASH OFFER

Step 2 - Ship Your Equipment

Shipping insurance is included at no additional cost to you. Your equipment will be insured for 100% of your cash offer quote. To meet shipping insurance eligibility, packages must be shipped with 3-4 inches of padding on all sides. A secure, tightly packed box with no loose items will help prevent any damage to the equipment during shipment. CLICK HERE to view our detailed shipping instructions.

Step 3 - Get Paid

Once we receive your equipment as described, we will process your payment in 1-2 business days. Get paid electronically via PayPal, or request a check in the mail. Checks may be expedited upon request for an additional cost.

2. How much is my equipment worth?

Simply CLICK HERE to request your cash offer. Our Cash Offer form will ask for basic info about your equipment, such as make, model and condition.

3. How would I get paid?

You can choose to get paid electronically via PayPal, or request a check in the mail. Payments are processed 1-2 business days after we receive your equipment as described. Checks may be expedited upon request for an additional cost.

4. How long is my cash offer quote good for?

Once you receive your cash offer quote, it is valid for 30 days. We want to make sure you enough time to finalize your decision and send us your device. If you need more time, contact us for an extension.

5. How does shipping work?

We will provide you with a FREE prepaid FedEx shipping label (insurance included) and shipping instructions.

6. How should I pack my item for shipping?

CLICK HERE to view our shipping instructions. To meet shipping insurance eligibility, packages must be shipped with 3-4 inches of padding on all sides. A secure, tightly packed box with no loose items will help prevent any damage to the equipment during shipment.

7. Where can I go to ship my equipment?

To find your nearest FedEx location to drop-off your package or to receieve professional shipping help, visit www.fedex.com/Locate.

8. How long is my shipping label good for?

FedEx will accept your shipping label up to 30 days after the ship date listed on the label.

9. Can CashForCameras mail me a box for me to ship my equipment?

At this time we are not mailing boxes. If you need shipping materials such as boxes, tape and bubble wrap, we highly recommend FedEx Office. To find your nearest FedEx Office or drop-off location, simply visit www.fedex.com/Locate or call 1.800.GoFedEx

10. What happens if my equipment gets damaged when I ship it?

All shipments are insured via Fedex, for 100% of your cash offer amount. If your equipment arrives broken or with major damage that you were not aware of, we will file a shipping insurance claim on your behalf and do everything we can to get you paid in FULL. CashForCameras will review how the item was packaged and will solely determine if your claim is eligible for payment. If your item was properly packaged, then the shipping insurance claim will be approved, and you will be paid in full for your original cash offer.

11. What happens if the equipment I am selling is determined to be “not as described” by CashForCameras?

If your equipment is determined to be "not as described”, we will email you and tell you how it differs from your description. We will give you an updated quote based upon the actual condition. We will even include photos of any damage. Our costumers normally agree with our revised quote as we always strive to be absolutely fair. If you are not satisfied, we can return the item to you at no additional cost.

12. Can I trust your website?

We have over 17 years of positive customer feedback. We have paid out over $1.75 Million Dollars to customers like you across America. Since 2002, we have earned over 5000 positive customer reviews! CLICK HERE to read some of our customer reviews.

13. Other than cameras, lenses, drone, and video equipment, are there any other products that cashForCameras is willing to buy?

Yes! We also will buy your Macbook, MacBook Pro, Macbook Air, iMac, iMac Pro, Mac Pro, Mac Mini Desktops, iPad, iPhone, Apple Watch, and LG UltraFine Display. Please visit our website CashForYourMac.com for more information.

14. If another website offers me more cash for my equipment, will CashForCameras match the offer?

Yes, We will do our best to match or beat offers from reputable camera buyers such as Adorama and B&H. Simply respond to our email offer with a copy of your higher offer. Please note we will not match eBay, Amazon or private party offers.

15. If my equipment is broken or not working, will CashForCameras still buy it?

No, we do not purchase equipment that is broken or not working.

16. What if I change my mind and want my equipment back, after I have already shipped?

As long as you have not yet receieved payment for your equipment, you can cancel the sale and request the return of your equipment. We will even cover the return shipping cost. Simply email us at support@cashforcameras.com to arrange the return of your equipment.
